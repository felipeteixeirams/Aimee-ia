## 2025-05-24 - [Accessible Icon Actions]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons in mobile-first layouts are often missing metadata, making them inaccessible to screen reader users who rely on the same compact UI. In high-interaction states like "Modo Compra", this can completely block users from essential functionality.
**Action:** Always provide descriptive `aria-label` attributes for icon-only buttons and interactive status indicators.
